## What Klaro does for you

**Instant answers, around the clock.** Your agent handles common questions automatically — even outside business hours — so customers don't wait and your team isn't buried in repetitive tickets.

**Trained on your content.** Upload PDFs, paste URLs, or add text directly. Klaro turns your existing documentation into a knowledge base your agent draws from — no restructuring, no special formatting needed.

**On-brand from day one.** Customize colors, name, icon, and tone to match your site. Your customers see your brand, not a generic chat window.

**Your team stays in control.** Every conversation lands in a shared inbox. Step in to reply as a human whenever you need to — or let the agent escalate automatically when it can't help.
